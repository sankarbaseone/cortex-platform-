# GAP-001: Missing standalone PRR/SRA document
Status: Accepted
## Affected RFC/ECD
`04-itp/NYDUX-ITP-01-freeze-certificate.md` (asserts PRR/SRA as part of the frozen corpus); `02-ecd/ECD-012-security-construction.md` (one line referencing a PRR finding).
## The ambiguity
The freeze certificate declares "PRR / SRA — Production Readiness Review — verdict APPROVED FOR IMPLEMENTATION, overall 9.2/10" as frozen and canonical, but no such document exists anywhere in the specification source — not on disk, not in any of the six ZIP archives searched.
## Reason
Not recoverable from the frozen specification archives.
## Resolution
Accepted as a permanent gap. Implementation authority for anything the PRR would otherwise have governed derives from the RFCs, the ECDs, and the ITP instead. See `docs/specification/v1.0/03-prr/README.md`.
## Narrowest-reading fallback
Treat `ECD-012` through `ECD-015` plus the ITP corpus as sufficient authority for security/testing/performance/execution guidance; do not block implementation on PRR recovery.
